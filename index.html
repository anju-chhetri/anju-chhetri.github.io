About

Hello, my name is Anju Chhetri.

I am an engineering graduate and currently a research assistant at Multimodal Learning Lab at NAAMII. My current research interests are in building more robust and reliable machine learning models. In the past, I have worked in the domain of adversarial machine learning, active learning and computer vision. In general, my passion centers around understanding the inner workings of machine learning systems.

When I'm not working on research, I enjoy being surrounded by nature, often going for hikes. The calming melodies of my ukulele are another way for me to relax and find inspiration.
