About

Hi, I am Anju Chhetri!

I am a senior year engineering student at Pulchowk Campus - Institute of engineering, Nepal. Presently, I am working in the domain of adversarial machine learning, looking for ways to design a strong adversarial attack for Latent Diffusion Models (LDMs). In general, my passion centers around understanding the inner workings of machine learning systems, and I find particular joy in understanding the intricate mathematical foundations that govern these complex yet captivating systems.
